    Hi, I'm Seth — an Android Developer who genuinely loves building mobile experiences that people enjoy using!

    With over two years of experience designing, developing, and maintaining innovative Android apps, I’ve found my happy place blending creativity, tech, and problem-solving — all while turning coffee into clean, functional code.

    At Ropali Corporation, I’ve been the sole Android developer. Yep, it’s been a one-person show — leading the charge on creating and improving apps across multiple departments:

    • Developed a POS system for a dinosaur-themed park (yes, that exists!)
    • Built mobile apps for agricultural teams with real-time tracking, barcode scanning, and CameraX image capture
    • Led full UI/UX revamps to make apps intuitive and visually appealing

    I love projects where I can:

    • Take ownership and build solutions from the ground up
    • Mix Java and Kotlin with grace (and minimal caffeine panic!)
    • Make life easier for sales teams, farmers, or even theme park visitors

    Before that, I explored Kotlin Multiplatform Mobile (KMM) and Jetpack Compose at AIX, collaborating in Agile teams and building scalable, cross-platform architectures.

    I'm a self-starter, a one-person Android army when needed — but I thrive in collaborative environments. Whether it’s geeking out over smooth animations, clean UI, or the thrill of seeing CI/CD run perfectly, I love crafting mobile apps that solve real-world problems.

    If you’re passionate about building people-centered apps, we’re already on the same page. Let’s create something awesome together!
